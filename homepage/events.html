The Lunar New Year Parade

The streets were awash with vibrant colors and the air filled with the sounds of celebration. Flushing's Lunar New Year parade is a spectacle of cultural pride and joy, drawing people from all walks of life to witness the dragon dances, traditional music, and the shared happiness of the community.
